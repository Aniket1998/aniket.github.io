Hello World!

I’m a Sophomore Undergrad in the Department of Electrical Engineering, IIT Kanpur fascinated by artificial intelligence. My academic interests currently lie in deep learning applied to computer vision. I’m highly passionate about unsupervised representation learning (particularly disentangled representations) and generative models, and spend quite a bit of my time tinkering with Generative Adversarial Networks and Variational Autoencoders. I also maintain an interest in the theory of Probabilistic Machine Learning

I also love contributing to the open source deep learning community, reading and understanding research papers in the field that pique my interest, then attempting to reproduce and marignally improve the results by writing in PyTorch and Tensorflow.

Unsupervised Representation Learning on Video Data

Working on deep generative models for learning meaningful latent disentangled representations from video data. Currently working on Disentangled Variational Autoencoders that can learn distinct variables for time invariant and temporal aspects of video data allowing disentangling of content and motion features. The disentanglement is performed only through clever design of the probabilistic graphical model without any modifications to the ELBO objective

TorchGAN

TorchGAN is a lightweight framework built on top of PyTorch for training and evaluating Generative Adversarial Networks
