About Me

I’m a Fourth Year Undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IITK), Double Majoring in Mathematics and Electrical Engineering. My research interests lie in the mathematical foundations of Machine Learning, with a focus on Optimization, High Dimensional Statistics, and Approximate Inference. I also maintain an active interest in continuous-time Machine Learning with ODEs and SDEs, and Deep Generative Models.

Since my Sophomore Year, I’ve been working as a Research Assistant in Prof. Piyush Rai’s group at IITK, focusing on Approximate Inference and Deep Generative Models. My previous work under Prof. Rai’s group involved developing Generative Models for Joint Image and Video Generation, and nonparametric priors for GANs using Second-Order ODEs. Currently, I am working on developing an extension of the Model Agnostic Meta-Learning (MAML) Algorithm for meta-learning of continuous-time dynamical systems, and investigating results from SDE theory to improve the performance of Stochastic Gradient MCMC Algorithms.

I spent my Third Year as an Exchange Student at Aalto University School of Science, Finland, where I worked as a Research Assistant under Dr. Markus Heinonen and Prof. Harri Lahdesmaki, focusing on Approximate Bayesian Inference and Adversarial Learning for continuous-time generative models governed by ODEs and SDEs.

I’m currently looking for Research Assistantship and collaboration opportunities in Optimization, Approximate Inference, High Dimensional Statistics, and Learning Theory. If you’re interested in a collaboration or have an opening in your research group where I could be of some use, please feel free to contact me.
